# Enterprise GRC Program & Risk Management Framework – SecurityGroup101

## Overview

This project simulates a full-scale Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) consulting engagement for a fintech organization. It demonstrates the design and implementation of an enterprise GRC program aligned to industry frameworks and regulatory requirements.

SecurityGroup101 is a growing fintech company operating cloud-based financial platforms and payment services. As the organization scaled, key challenges emerged:

- Fragmented risk management processes  
- Inconsistent control implementation  
- Limited visibility into enterprise risk posture  
- Increasing regulatory and cybersecurity exposure  

This project addresses those challenges through a structured, risk-based GRC program.

---

## Objectives

- Establish an enterprise-wide risk management framework  
- Quantify and prioritize risk using ALE, SLE, and ARO  
- Align controls across multiple compliance frameworks  
- Design governance structures and accountability models  
- Improve organizational resilience through DRP and BCP integration  

---

## Frameworks and Standards

This program aligns with:

- NIST SP 800-30 (Risk Assessment)  
- NIST SP 800-34 (Disaster Recovery and Contingency Planning)  
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)  
- ISO 27001  
- COBIT 2019  
- GLBA, PCI DSS, FFIEC, SOX  

---

## Key Capabilities

### Risk Management
- Built a quantitative risk assessment model using ALE, SLE, and ARO  
- Developed a risk register prioritizing assets by financial impact  
- Created a risk heatmap to support risk-based decision-making  

### Compliance and Control Mapping
- Conducted GLBA gap analysis with remediation recommendations  
- Developed a PCI DSS crosswalk mapped to NIST, ISO, and COBIT  
- Aligned controls with FFIEC cybersecurity expectations  
- Built a unified control catalog across multiple frameworks  

### Governance
- Designed a governance model aligned to COBIT 2019 and ISO 38500  
- Defined roles, responsibilities, and risk ownership  
- Established escalation and reporting processes  

### Resilience and Recovery
- Developed a Disaster Recovery Plan aligned to NIST SP 800-34  
- Built a Business Continuity Plan for operational continuity  
- Created a DR and BCP testing schedule  

### Program Development
- Designed an 18-month GRC roadmap from assessment to maturity  
- Integrated risk, compliance, governance, and resilience into a single program  

---

## Business Impact

- Enables risk-based prioritization of security investments  
- Improves visibility into enterprise risk exposure  
- Strengthens regulatory alignment for fintech operations  
- Enhances resilience against cyber and operational disruptions  
- Supports audit readiness and compliance maturity
