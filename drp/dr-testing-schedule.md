# DR and BCP Testing Schedule – SecurityGroup101

A structured testing program ensures that **Disaster Recovery (DRP)** and **Business Continuity (BCP)** procedures remain effective, validated, and aligned with the current operating environment.

This schedule supports resilience, regulatory compliance, and continuous improvement.

---

## Objectives

- Validate recovery capabilities across critical systems and processes  
- Ensure alignment with defined **RTO and RPO targets**  
- Test coordination between technical recovery and business continuity functions  
- Identify gaps and improve response readiness  

---

## Testing Matrix

| Test Type | Purpose | Frequency | Owner |
|----------|--------|----------|-------|
| **Tabletop Exercise** | Validate roles, decision-making, and communication workflows | Semiannual | Disaster Recovery Coordinator |
| **Backup Restore Test** | Validate backup integrity and data restorability | Monthly | Backup and Data Recovery Lead |
| **Technical Simulation** | Validate system recovery procedures and technical workflows | Semiannual | IT Systems Lead |
| **Full Failover Test** | Validate end-to-end disaster recovery capability | Annual | IT Infrastructure and Executive Leadership |
| **Communication Test** | Validate alerting, escalation, and notification processes | Quarterly | Communications Lead |

---

## Post-Test Activities

- Document test results, issues, and observed gaps  
- Capture lessons learned and improvement opportunities  
- Update DRP, BCP, and operational runbooks  
- Report outcomes to executive leadership and relevant stakeholders  

---

## Key Insights

- Regular testing is essential to ensure recovery procedures remain effective and executable  
- Backup validation is critical to successful recovery during ransomware or data loss events  
- Coordination between technical teams and business units improves response efficiency  
- Communication testing ensures timely and accurate information flow during incidents  

---

## Business Impact

This testing program supports:

- Reduced recovery time and improved operational resilience  
- Increased confidence in disaster recovery and continuity capabilities  
- Alignment with regulatory expectations (**NIST SP 800-34, FFIEC**)  
- Continuous improvement of incident response and recovery processes  

---

## Summary

This testing schedule establishes a repeatable and measurable approach to validating disaster recovery and business continuity capabilities.

It ensures that SecurityGroup101 maintains a high level of preparedness and resilience in the face of cyber and operational disruptions.
